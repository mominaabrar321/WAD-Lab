Seasons

Spring

Spring is a beautiful season that comes after winter. The weather becomes pleasant, the days are bright, and flowers bloom in many colors. Trees grow new green leaves, and birds sing sweet songs. Everywhere looks fresh and full of life.

People enjoy spring because it is neither too hot nor too cold. Children like to play outside, and families visit parks and gardens. Farmers are happy to see their fields turning green with new crops. Spring is truly the season of happiness and hope.

Summer

Summer is the hottest season of the year. The days are long, the sun shines brightly, and the weather is warm. People enjoy eating cold ice creams, drinking lemonade, and wearing light clothes. Many children get summer holidays from school and spend their time playing or visiting relatives.

Although the heat can be strong, summer is also a fun season. Families go to beaches, swimming pools, and parks to enjoy the sunshine. Fruits like mangoes, melons, and watermelons are available, making summer even more exciting. That is why summer is called the season of fun and energy.
